🌱 Plant Troubleshooting Guide

Search for your gardening issue and find solutions!

🌿 Yellow Leaves

Possible Causes: Overwatering, nitrogen deficiency, poor drainage.

Fix: Reduce watering, check for proper drainage, add nitrogen-rich fertilizer.

🐛 Pests on Plants

Possible Causes: Aphids, caterpillars, fungal infections.

Fix: Use neem oil spray, introduce ladybugs, trim affected leaves.

🌾 Poor Growth

Possible Causes: Lack of sunlight, soil nutrient depletion, overcrowding.

Fix: Move to a sunnier spot, use compost, thin out crowded plants.
