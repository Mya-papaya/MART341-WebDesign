Materials and Tools

Here are the essential supplies you'll need to get started with oil painting:

  • Oil Paint Set: A beginner-friendly set with pre-selected colors is ideal for mixing and experimenting.
  • Canvas Panels: Affordable and small-sized panels are perfect for practice without the pressure of ruining expensive materials.
  • Brush Set: Include a variety of shapes like flat, round, and filbert brushes for different techniques.
  • Mediums: Linseed oil for blending and gamsol for thinning paint are great options.
  • Palette: Use a sturdy palette for mixing colors, or try a palette saver to keep paints fresh.
  • Palette Knives: Useful for mixing paints and creating textured effects.
  • Easel: A simple tabletop easel works well for beginners.
  • Cleaning Supplies: Soap and warm water for brushes, and paper towels for quick cleanups.
