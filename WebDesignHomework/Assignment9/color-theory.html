Understanding Color

Mastering color theory can transform your oil painting. Here are some key concepts to get you started:

  • Warm vs. Cool Colors: Warm tones like red and orange create a sense of energy, while cool tones like blue and green evoke calm and tranquility.
  • Complementary Colors: Colors opposite each other on the color wheel, such as blue and orange, create striking contrasts when used together.
  • Analogous Colors: Colors next to each other on the color wheel, like red, orange, and yellow, produce harmonious effects.
  • Color Harmony: A limited palette helps maintain balance and prevents overwhelming your composition.
  • Tonal Values: Light and dark variations in color add depth and dimension to your painting.
