Vegetarian Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, sauté onion and zucchini until softened. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Layer the lasagna: Start with marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, followed by noodles, ricotta cheese, sautéed vegetables, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with Parmesan cheese and bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
